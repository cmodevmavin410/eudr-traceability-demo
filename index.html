From farmer self-audit to EU export — a click-through demo of a deforestation-free supply chain

● DEMO · SAMPLE DATA
Compliance Marketplace

See how one plot of land becomes an EU-compliant, fully traceable product

This interactive demo walks through the complete journey a smallholder farmer's harvest takes to reach the EU market under the EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR) — and shows how a single platform, built from two connected modules, makes it possible:

Marketplace  Farmer sideSelf-audit · product listing · selling
Compliance  Verification sideMapping · deforestation check · certificate · traceability

You'll play the whole flow in 7 steps. The map, the GeoJSON file and the QR code are fully functional; the satellite deforestation check is simulated for the demo.

Marketplace · Farmer
STEP 1 OF 7

Farmer self-audit

The farmer Somchai Jaidee signs in to the Marketplace app and self-declares against the three core EUDR conditions. No auditor visit is required for EUDR — unlike FSC, which needs an independent Certification Body. Tick all three to continue.
